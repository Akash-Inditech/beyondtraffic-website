SECTION FAQ- BEYOND TRAFFIC

1)What is a people counting system?
A people counting system is a technology solution that tracks the number of visitors entering and exiting a store or location. Beyond Traffic uses Al-powered sensors and footfall analytics software to provide real-time visitor insights, helping businesses in the UAE make data-driven decisions.
2)How does footfall analytics help retail businesses in the UAE?
Footfall analytics helps UAE retailers understand:
  • How many customers visit their store?
  • When peak hours occur
  • How many visitors convert into buyers?
This allows businesses to optimize staffing, improve store layouts, and increase sales performance.
3)Is Beyond Traffic suitable for malls and large retail chains in Dubai?
Yes. Beyond Traffic is designed for:
  • Multi-store retail chains
  • Shopping malls
  • Large commercial spaces
It provides centralized dashboards to compare performance across multiple locations in Dubai and across the UAE.
4)How accurate is the Beyond Traffic people counting system?
Beyond Traffic uses advanced 3D stereo vision sensors with Al filtering technology, delivering up to 98%+ accuracy under typical retail conditions. It eliminates false counts caused by shadows, reflections, and non-human objects.
5)Can the system differentiate between staff and customers?
Yes. Beyond Traffic can distinguish staff from customers, ensuring accurate footfall data and more reliable conversion rate analysis.
6) Does the system track customer demographics?
Yes. The platform provides basic demographic insights such as:
  • Gender (male/female)
  • Adults vs children
  • Group vs individual visitors
This helps UAE retailers tailor marketing and in-store experiences.
7)Can Beyond Traffic measure conversion rates?
Yes. By integrating with your POS system, Beyond Traffic compares footfall data with transactions to calculate conversion rates and identify opportunities to improve sales.
8)What kind of reports does the system provide?
Beyond Traffic offers:
  • Real-time dashboards
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly reports
  • Automated email reports
  • Customizable analytics views
Reports can be accessed anytime from cloud-based dashboards.
9)Can I monitor multiple stores from one dashboard?
Yes. Beyond Traffic provides multi-store analytics, allowing you to monitor performance across all your UAE locations from a single platform.

The cost depends on:
  • Number of stores

  • Required features and integrations
