Fashion & Apparel Retail

From high-street fashion to independent boutiques — fashion retailers in the UAE need to know exactly who's walking through the door, when, and how often they convert to buyers.

Challenge:

High rent = high pressure to convert every visitor

Solution:

  • Know which displays attract the most dwell time
  • Compare conversion rates across mall locations
  • Staff peak hours based on real footfall data
  • Understand gender split to optimize stock mix

Shopping Malls & Retail Centres

UAE shopping malls are among the busiest in the world. Mall operators need accurate zone-by-zone footfall data to serve tenants and manage operations at scale.

  • Entrance-by-entrance traffic counts across the mall
  • Zone-level footfall for tenant benchmarking
  • Occupancy counter for safety & compliance
  • Event impact measurement (DSF sales tracking)
  • Automated reports for tenant reporting
Zone Analytics Occupancy Counter Tenant Reports

Jewellery & Luxury Retail

Jewellery retail in the UAE is high-value and high-service. Know your true window-to-visit rate and track if your displays are pulling people in.

  • Pass-by to entry conversion rate (window appeal metric)
  • Sales conversion from footfall vs. appointments
  • Gender demographics for targeted marketing
  • Staff-to-customer ratio optimization

Hospitality & F&B

Hotels, restaurants, cafes and food courts in the UAE can use footfall analytics to manage queue times, optimize seating, and track peak periods.

  • Live occupancy counter for seating management
  • Peak traffic hours for kitchen scheduling
  • Queue monitoring at reception/buffet
  • Group visit identification for reservations
